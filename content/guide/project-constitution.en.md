## Purpose and scope

Software Architecture Atlas connects project requirements to languages, architectures, frameworks, AI systems, standards, RFCs, and software development practices through reasoned assessments.

## Content principles

- Canonical content lives in Markdown, YAML, and JSON.
- Claims should link to official or academic sources whenever possible.
- Document review status and technology maturity are tracked separately.
- AI-assisted drafts are not reviewed or verified until human review is complete.
- Turkish and English documents are separate but linked by `translationKey`.
